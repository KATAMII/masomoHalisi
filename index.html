Masomo Halisi College

Welcome to Masomo Halisi College, a leading institution of higher learning in Murang'a County, Kenya. We offer a variety of courses in fields such as business, education, engineering, health sciences, and information technology. Our mission is to provide quality education that is relevant, accessible, and affordable to all students.


We have a team of qualified and experienced faculty, staff, and administrators who are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community service. We also have modern facilities, equipment, and resources that enhance the learning experience of our students.


Whether you are a prospective or current student, a parent, a partner, or a visitor, we invite you to explore our website and discover the opportunities and benefits of studying at Masomo Halisi College. Thank you for choosing us as your partner in education.

How We Make Your Education Better

We offer scholarship factilitation to the needy and bright students, online classes for those who want to study from anywhere and certifications that are recognized everywhere around the globe.

Schorlaship Facility

We offer various scholarships and bursaries to students who qualify based on academic performance, financial need, or other factors. Apply online or contact our financial aid office for details.

Online Courses

We partner with Dell Technologies to provide you with online courses that enhance your skills and knowledge in the latest technologies. Learn from industry experts, earn certificates, and boost your employability.

Global Certification

We help you obtain internationally recognized certifications that validate your skills and knowledge in various fields. You can choose from a range of certification programs offered by our partners and prepare for the exams with our guidance.
